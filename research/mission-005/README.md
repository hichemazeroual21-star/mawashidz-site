# Mission 005 — The Algerian Traveler's Pain Map

Ultra-deep behavioral and operational research on problems Algerian travelers face before, during, and after booking travel.

## Deliverable

- **[ALGERIAN_TRAVELER_PAIN_MAP.md](./ALGERIAN_TRAVELER_PAIN_MAP.md)** — Full evidence-based report

## Contents

1. Top 100 recurring traveler problems (frequency, severity, who, solutions, failure modes, evidence)
2. Emotional journey (dreaming → returning home)
3. Trust analysis (agencies, confidence, pre-payment fears)
4. Opportunity map (55 unmet needs)
5. Hidden opportunities (underserved but repeatedly reported)
6. Executive summary (one-product recommendation)
7. References (40+ primary sources)

## Scope covered

Airline tickets · Travel agencies · Visas · Hotels · Packages · Umrah/Hajj · International & domestic · Family · Student · Medical · Business

## Method note

Claims are labeled **FACT** vs **INTERPRETATION**. Frequency scores are evidence-weighted (not national survey percentages). Social-platform complaints are triangulated via investigative journalism and official alerts where raw threads are ephemeral.
