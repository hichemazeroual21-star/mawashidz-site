## The Market That Exists

**INTERPRETATION:** The addressable market is **Outbound Administrative Eligibility Services** — helping Algerian persons prove they qualify for foreign permission to travel, plus lawful appointment access signals.

**FACT anchor:** 544k+ Schengen applications (M007); documentary causes ~48% of cited refusals (M005 #34–37).

**NOT the market:** "Algerian travel booking" ($X OTA TAM fiction).
