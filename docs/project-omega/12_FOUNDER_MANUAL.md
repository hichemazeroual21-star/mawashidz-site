# 12 — FOUNDER'S OPERATING MANUAL (10-Year)
## Project Omega | Phase 12

---

## 1. Identity Rules

1. We are **WATHIQ** — eligibility intelligence. Never call ourselves travel startup.
2. We **never hold money** for customers or agencies.
3. We **never promise** visas, tickets, or refunds we don't control.

## 2. Product Rules

4. Every product must reduce **document uncertainty** or **lawful information asymmetry**.
5. No product ships without **cited official rule source** in output.
6. AI **extracts**; rules **decide**; humans **certify** (Year 1–2).
7. Free tools must feed paid **eligibility** — not engagement vanity.
8. One country / one visa type until **500 paid reviews** with NPS >40.

## 3. Research Rules

9. Label FACT / INTERPRETATION / ASSUMPTION / UNKNOWN on every strategy doc.
10. Missions 005–007 are **canonical evidence**; later missions are hypotheses.
11. No strategy meeting without **one new unknown** logged to `15_UNKNOWNS.md`.
12. Annual re-validation of Madouche 2018 stats — **survey or kill claims**.

## 4. AI Rules

13. No approval probability scores — **ever**.
14. Log every model version against outcome dataset.
15. Human override button on every user-facing recommendation.
16. Darija support before English polish.

## 5. Hiring Rules

17. First hire after PMF: **French-speaking dossier reviewer** (not engineer).
18. No hire increases burn without **+20% revenue** trailing 90 days.
19. Industry hire (ex-agency) before MBA.
20. Red team review quarterly — external skeptic.

## 6. Partnership Rules

21. Agencies: white-label only; never compete for end customer.
22. Government: ask for **data**, not **endorsement**, first.
23. Yassir: partner or sell — never race.
24. Insurers: integration for conformity (#35), not underwriting.

## 7. Expansion Rules

25. Adjacent country only when France **NPS >50** and **churn <30%**.
26. No inbound tourism until outbound PMF + BA tourist wallet clarity.
27. No Gulf until ONPO partnership exists.

## 8. Kill Rules

28. Shut down if E001 conversion <2% after 1000 qualified leads.
29. Shut down Signal if legal C&D without partnership path.
30. Pivot to services agency if AI commoditized and outcome data empty.

## 9. Financial Rules

31. Bootstrap default; VC only for **data moat** financing, not marketing.
32. 18-month runway minimum before any hire.
33. Success-fee Recover funds ops — not dossier loss-leader forever.

## 10. Founder Psychology Rules

34. **Consistency is not truth** — update when evidence changes.
35. **Optimism is not strategy** — see Mission 009 quarterly.
36. If product excites founders more than customers, **stop**.
