# SECTION 5 — THE FIRST MVP

## What It Is

**Not a feature list.** A single behavioral intervention at the moment of maximum fear.

### Core Problem
**FACT (M005, M006):** Travelers pay agencies and airlines before service is confirmed. Money enters a black hole. Confidence is lowest post-payment. ~90% of agencies cannot be distinguished by consumers.

### Core Promise
> **"Your money moves only when your trip is real."**

Pay through us. Funds release when your agency proves delivery (contract signed → PNR issued → visa obtained → you depart). If they fail, you don't lose.

### Target User
**Primary:** Algerian resident family booking France/Europe trip via physical agency (obligation + aspiration archetype, M006).  
**Secondary:** Family member abroad (diaspora) funding or monitoring the journey.

### Expected Behavior
1. **Discover** agency via referral, Facebook, or our verified directory
2. **Verify** agency license on platform (30 seconds)
3. **Initiate** escrow payment via SATIM instead of cash transfer
4. **Track** milestones on family dashboard (WhatsApp alerts to siblings/parents)
5. **Release** funds as agency hits milestones (PNR upload triggers partial release)
6. **Resolve** disputes via platform if agency fails (72h SLA)
7. **Review** post-trip (verified only)

### Why Users Switch
| From | To Us Because |
|------|---------------|
| Cash to agency | M005 #16: agency vanishes; escrow protects |
| Direct Air Algérie payment | M005 #1: debit without ticket; we hold until PNR |
| Cousin recommendation only | M006: we formalize trust with license + complaint data |
| Facebook scam deals | M005: verified agencies only in directory |
